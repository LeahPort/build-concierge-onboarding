Welcome to Build Concierge

Your structured onboarding journey starts here.

Over the next four weeks, we will set you up for long term success with a clear plan, shared responsibilities and measurable progress.

Our Goal

To help your team become confident, capable and self-sufficient users of the Build Concierge platform so you start seeing real value fast.

Your Four Week Onboarding Programme

Your onboarding includes everything required to get you live and delivering instant results.

Platform Setup

  • Account creation
  • Login details issued
  • Site configuration and access approved

Workflow Configuration

  • Guided configuration of two to three workflows
  • One-to-one training sessions
  • All workflows tested with you before going live

Integrations

  • BigChange setup or API configuration
  • Channel setup across WhatsApp, phone and email
  • Concierge Receptionist setup where applicable

Training & Resources

  • Live training sessions with our team
  • Printable quick start guides
  • Full access to the knowledge base
  • Training recordings for your team

Onboarding Process: Step-by-Step

1

Step 1: Setup & Workflow 1 Kickoff

Customer

  • Sales to onboarding handover
  • Provide details for two workflows

Build Concierge

  • Introduce Support team
  • Support process mapping for workflows
  • Training session on building workflow one
  • Daily monitoring
2

Step 2: Workflow 1 Sign-Off & Workflow 2 Kickoff

Customer

  • Test workflow one
  • Sign off workflow one

Build Concierge

  • Training session on workflow two
  • Daily monitoring
3

Step 3: Go-Live Preparations & Sign-Off

Customer

  • Sign off workflow two
  • Sign off UAT (User Acceptance Testing)
  • Agree plan for future feature rollouts

Build Concierge

  • Reconfirm Support options and future timeframes
✓

Post Go Live Support (30 Day Hypercare)

  • A 30 day hypercare period with priority access
  • Weekly check ins
  • Handover to the Customer Success or Support team
  • A best practice call after 30 days
  • Access to our resource library and customer community

Pre Onboarding Preparation

Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide BigChange API details
  • Appoint a Superuser
  • Confirm user accounts and domains
  • Sign off on site setup and access
  • Confirm any stakeholder annual leave

Build Concierge Responsibilities

  • Create your account
  • Share login details
  • Book weekly progress calls
  • Agree communication channels
  • Provide product overview
  • Confirm Support team availability

Key Contacts: A dedicated team across Support, Product and Customer Success will work with you throughout onboarding.
